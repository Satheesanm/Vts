Event Overview

Register to participate in this event on October 13th to October 14th. The 5th annual Halifax Innovation Challenge is here! This is a chance to build meaningful skills and provides a networking experience for post-secondary students in Nova Scotia.

The Opportunity

Solve real business challenges and demonstrate your skills. Explore dynamic career opportunities. Network with senior leaders and learn from technology and innovation experts.

The Reward

A cash prize of $1200 for the winning team with many other small prizes to be awarded! This is your chance to showcase your skills and make an impact.
